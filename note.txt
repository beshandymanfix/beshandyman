The Ideal Architecture for Your Stack

PM2 = Process Manager 2
EC2 = Elastic Compute Cloud

1. cloudflare: point to your EC2 IP address

2. AWS EC2" Runs your next.js app continuosly using PM2 

3. MongoDB/S3: Your Next.js app connects to these via standard env variables. 

Why it's good: Extremely fast Server-Side Rendering (great for SEO), highly predictable, and cheap to start.
